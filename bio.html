SPIKE JONZE

Bio

Spike Jonze (born Adam Spiegel in New York City) is one of the most original and creative filmmaker of his generation. Jonze started his career directing some of the coolest skateboarding videos ever made. He then quickly became a very prolific music video director and made many iconic music videos in the late 90's and early 00's. He made his film feature debut in 1999 with "Being John Malkovich" and since then, he directed many bold and interesting films. Spike Jonze's body of work is definitely worth discovering for those of you who never had the chance to.
